Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kaiber better than Luma Dream Machine?

Kaiber is better for artistic, stylized content like music videos. Luma is better for cinematic realism and photorealistic AI video at a lower cost.

What is Kaiber best for?

Kaiber excels at creating stylized AI videos, music videos, and art-direction-heavy content where you want a distinctive visual style rather than photorealism.

Which AI video tool is best for music videos?

Kaiber is the top choice for music videos thanks to its audio-reactive features and stylized AI generation. Luma works well for cinematic inserts but lacks Kaiber's music-specific features.
