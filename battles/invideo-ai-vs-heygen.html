InVideo AI vs HeyGen

Text To VideoAvatars
✓ Updated March 2026
The 2026 Verdict

Pick InVideo AI if you want to crank out faceless YouTube and promo videos from a prompt with stock footage doing most of the visual work. Pick HeyGen if your content needs a realistic on-screen presenter and you care more about avatar delivery than montage-style edits.

Category InVideo AI HeyGen Winner
